ZappaStream Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ZappaStream is a radio player app for the Zappateers 24/7 Frank Zappa stream. This policy describes what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data we do not collect

ZappaStream does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Data stored locally on your device

  • Listen history and favourites — shows you have listened to and marked as favourite are stored locally using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device.
  • App preferences — settings such as your chosen stream format, text size, and audio FX values are stored in standard system preferences (UserDefaults) on your device only.
  • DVR buffer — when the DVR feature is active, a short rolling audio buffer (up to 15 minutes) is written to your device's temporary storage. It is deleted when the app is closed or the buffer is cleared.

Network requests

ZappaStream connects to the following external services solely to provide app functionality:

  • shoutcast.norbert.de — to stream audio and retrieve now-playing metadata.
  • zappateers.com — to retrieve setlist and show information for the currently playing concert.
  • donlope.net — to look up additional track information when you tap the "Track Info" button. This is optional and only triggered by your action.

No identifying information is sent to any of these services. Connections are standard HTTP/HTTPS requests indistinguishable from a web browser visit.

Children's privacy

ZappaStream does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy
